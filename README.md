## About

AnChat is a decentralized social chat application built on the [Orama Network](https://orama.network), an open-source peer-to-peer ecosystem.

**No email. No phone number. No data collection.** Create an account in seconds with the built-in wallet and start chatting — anyone can join.

## Features

- **Post-Quantum End-to-End Encryption** — Messages and group keys are protected with a hybrid X25519 + ML-KEM-768 scheme, resistant to future quantum attacks
- **Open Sign-Up** — Anyone can create an account. A built-in self-custodial wallet ([RootWallet](https://rootwallet.io)) is generated on the spot — no external wallet or approval needed
- **Built-in Multi-Chain Wallet** — Send & receive on **Solana, Ethereum, Base, and Bitcoin**, plus your NFTs. More networks coming as RootWallet expands
- **Direct Messages, Groups & Rooms** — 1-on-1 chats, group conversations, and topic-based rooms
- **Voice & Video Calls** — Encrypted P2P audio and HD video calling, including background & lock-screen incoming calls
- **Rich Messaging** — Replies, reactions, mentions, edits, read receipts, and in-chat search
- **Attachments** — Share photos, videos, voice memos, and documents
- **Stealth Mode** — One tap enables five privacy protections: Touch Reveal, Invite Only, Screenshot Block, Voice Mask, and Ghost Trail
- **Vanish** — Delete your account and data completely
- **Zero Data Collection** — Your keys and data never leave your control; nothing is monetized
- **Censorship Resistant** — Decentralized infrastructure with no central point of control

## Subscriptions

AnChat is funded by subscriptions — no ads, ever. Free, **Premium**, and **VIP** tiers unlock more storage, unlimited groups, and unlimited call minutes. Pay in **USDC** or **ANCHAT** on Solana. DeBros NFT holders get **lifetime** Premium or VIP access, unlocked automatically on sign-in.

See [premium.anchat.io](https://premium.anchat.io) for tiers and pricing.
